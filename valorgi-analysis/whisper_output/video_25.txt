Did you know there's an island in the U.S. that doesn't allow cars?
This is Mackenac Island in Michigan.
And in 1898, they banned all cars because they were scaring the horses.
Today, the main mode of transport is still horse and carriage.
And yes, that's even how Amazon packages get delivered.
Being car-free in such a car-centric country makes Mackenac Island very unique.
